Capture the data table in the document by clicking points on the document. Start by clicking the top left corner, and click points along the top line (corners are easiest), then click the matching points along the bottom of the document, going clockwise until you reach the bottom left corner.

You can drag and drop points by clicking around them. When you have an equal number of points on the top line and bottom line, you can click "Apply Dewarp" to have the program try and warp the table into a square image for easy transcription.

If the document doesn't end up very square, you can come back to the Dewarp tab and try again until it looks good.
